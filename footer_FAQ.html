  1. How do I create an account?

    You can create a new account by clicking on MY AREA, located in the upper right corner of the homepage. A new page will open, click on REGISTER NOW and fill out the form.

    To create an account, you must provide the following information: name, last name, email address and create a password. You can change your personal information at any time, this information is
    confidential and will not be sent to third parties.

  2. How can I change my password?

    To change your password, you must be logged in. Go to your account and click on the dedicated link to change your password. Fill out the form and click SAVE.

  3. Are the items sold on this website authentic?

    LuisaViaRoma is an authorized retailer for all brands present on the website and in our stores in Florence, Italy. The LUISAVIAROMA boutique and its official website offer genuine clothing, shoes and
    accessories from the most important designers and brands in fashion.

  4. Do sold out items get restocked?

    The LUISAVIAROMA website reflects the actual availability of items in our warehouses and stores.
    If a size is no longer available, it means that it is sold out. Unfortunately, we are unable to predict if an item will be restocked later in the season.
    We recommend that you periodically check the item page or the collection for new size availability or add the item to your WISHLIST to easily check its status.

  5. When will my order be shipped?

    Available items will be shipped within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday) following the order’s completion. During sales or special promotions, processing times may take longer.

  6. What is the cost of shipping?

    The method and cost of shipping vary depending on the selected shipping country. On the page SHIPMENT & FEES, you can find all relevant information by selecting the desired shipping country from the
    drop down menu at the top of the page.

  7. Can I cancel an order?

    Only orders that have not been processed can be cancelled.
    To request an order cancellation please go to MY AREA and either sign into ACCOUNT or insert the email used to place the order and the order number.
    If it is possible to cancel the order, you will see the button CANCEL ORDER.
    For more information, contact our customer service at the following email: customerservice@luisaviaroma.com

  8. How can I return an item?

    If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return unwanted items within 28 days of the order’s delivery date. Depending on your shipping country you may have the option to
    choose from one of the following return types: Size/Color Exchange, Store Credit or Refund.

    The Security Tag must still be undamaged, intact and attached to returned items.

    To request a return please go to MY AREA and either sign into your account or insert the email used to place the order and the order number. Select the order and items you want to return and choose the
    type of refund. Once completed, LuisaViaRoma will send a confirmation email, with the Return Authorization Number (RAN), the shipping label and instructions for the return shipment.

    For more information please click here.

  9. When will I receive my refund?

    Returns:
    Once your return has been received by the LUISAVIAROMA warehouse, and the items have been verified, we will process the return and you will receive a confirmation email.
    For orders paid for by Bank Wire Transfer, we ask that you respond to the email with the requested information.
    It may take up to 10 days for the refund to be visible on your account, due to bank processing time.

    Cancelled Orders:
    The refund will be processed as soon as you receive a confirmation email.
    It may take up to 10 days for the refund to be visible on your account, due to bank processing time.

  10. What do I do if I receive a defective item?

    We guarantee that all our items are verified by our quality control department before they are shipped. If you happen to receive a defective item, we ask you to send an email to
    customerservice@luisaviaroma.com specifying your order number and item code, including photos (showing the security tag visibly attached to the item) so that we can assess the defect.

    Please note that items that have been damaged due to normal wear and tear, improper use or by accident, are not considered defective items.

  11. What should I do if I don’t receive my order or if there are items missing from my order?

    In case the package does not arrive or there are items missing, you must keep the packaging so that we can establish what transpired.

    Any dispute regarding damages, missing items, or delivery to the wrong address must be communicated to LUISAVIAROMA within a maximum of 10 days from the delivery date shown on the courier’s
    website, otherwise it will not be accepted.

  12. Can I change the size and/or color of one or all the items of an order I have already completed?

    Unfortunately, it is not possible to change a completed order.

  13. Can I change the shipping address of an order I have already placed?

    Unfortunately it is not possible to change the shipping address once an order that has been completed.

  14. How can I check the status of my shipment?

    As soon as your order is shipped you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. By clicking on the tracking number you will be able to check the status of the shipment directly on the
    courier’s website.

    The order will also be visible on our website, in MY AREA, in ORDERS section.
