ORDER PAYMENT

  • CREDIT CARD
    When placing an order online customers are required to enter their credit card details into a protected online banking system provided by Banca Sella, WorldPay, Adyen and Braintree. Visa, MasterCard and American Express are accepted.
    For more information please visit: www.sella.it, www.worldpay.com, www.braintreepayments.com and www.adyen.com.

    1. CREDIT CARD SECURITY

      All transactions are processed through a secure server, guaranteeing customers on the website LUISAVIAROMA maximum credit card security.
      LUISAVIAROMA CUSTOMER SERVICE AGENTS WILL NEITHER ASK FOR NOR ACCEPT CREDIT CARD NUMBERS OR EXPIRY DATES.
      LUISAVIAROMA recommends customers to apply for e-commerce security PIN CODES provided by Verified by Visa and SecureCode by MasterCard.
      For more information please visit www.visa.com or www.mastercard.com

    2. CREDIT CARD CHARGES

      If there are sufficient funds and the account information is valid, the transaction will be processed instantly. At the moment the order is confirmed, the Credit Card will be charged for both available
      items and for Pre-Order items, which will be shipped later in the season when the merchandise arrives.

  • BANK WIRE TRANSFER
    When Bank Wire Transfer is selected as the payment method, customers will automatically receive a confirmation email containing LUISAVIAROMA bank details. Items will then be reserved until payment is
    confirmed. Customers are required to fax or email a scanned copy of the Bank Wire Transfer within 48 (forty-eight) hours the email request for payment.
    Customers are required to fax or email a scanned copy of the Bank Wire Transfer within 48 (forty-eight) hours in order to avoid order cancelation. Items will be dispatched once payment has reached the
    LUISAVIAROMA bank account.
    If the fax or scanned copy of the Bank Wire Transaction is not received within 48 (forty-eight) hours of request, relevant order will be cancelled.
    Items will be dispatched once LUISAVIAROMA has confirmed payment and as soon as items are available for shipment. This payment method is not available in all countries. Please check availability at
    checkout.
  • WECHAT PAY
    Customers who have downloaded the WeChat App may choose to pay via the WeChat Pay feature. For more information, please visit WeChat and WeChat Pay official websites: www.wechat.com and
    pay.weixin.qq.com/index.php/public/wechatpay
  • KLARNA
    Customers will be able to choose the Klarna service to pay for all of their orders.
    Registration and payment, as well as all thereto related policies can be found at www.klarna.com
    Please note that depending on the billing country Klarna may not be available as a payment method.
  • PAYPAL
    LUISAVIAROMA accepts payments from PayPal accounts and reserves the right to process orders only if dispatched to the same address that the verified PayPal account is registered with.
  • AFFIRM
    Customers choosing the Affirm service will be able to pay all of their orders in installments.
    Registration and payment as well as all thereto related policies can be found at www.affirm.com
  • FIRST-TIME CUSTOMERS
    We remind our new customers that LUISAVIAROMA may request a third-party supplier acting as an independent Data Controller to verify the transaction. Such process might potentially cause delays in
    shipping.
    LUISAVIAROMA also reserves the right to dispatch ordered items only to the Billing Address provided by customers.
